About Space4

Space 4 is a co-working and events space in the heart of Finsbury Park, giving digital co-operatives and those working in positive sectors a space to meet, work and host events.

All profit generated from Space 4 goes towards digital projects that make the world a fairer place.

Please note that unfortunately Space 4 is not wheelchair accessible.
