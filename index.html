About Space4

Space 4 is a co-working and events space in the heart of Finsbury Park, giving digital co-operatives and those working in positive sectors a space to meet, work and host events.

All profit generated from Space 4 goes towards digital projects that make the world a fairer place.

Please note that unfortunately Space 4 is not wheelchair accessible.

Desks

Space 4 includes 25 desks, which can be used on a short- or long-term basis.

Space 4 is not your run-of-the mill serviced coworking space. Instead it is run cooperatively, meaning that everyone working here shares responsibility for making it a success. This includes promoting the space and the people here, organising and attending events and maintaining it as a great place to work.

  • £200+VAT per month per desk - reductions are possible in exchange for payment in kind
  • Broadband internet access - Up to 350Mb Download + 20Mb Upload
  • 24/7 access Free use of meeting room (first come, first served booking)
  • Colour A4 + A3 printer
  • Projector available on request
  • Lockers available
  • Kitchen with microwave, kettle, running water, plates, cutlery etc.
  • Various social events with other residents and networks
  • We ask everyone who takes a desk to organise an event during their term here

Events Room

Our events room can host up to 50 people theatre style or standing and is also appropriate for smaller groups.

The events space can either be hired outright for your independent event. Additionally Outlandish works in partnership like-minded organisations to curate events including talks, workshops, hackdays, film screenings and more.

  • Day-time event 0-25 people: £500+VAT (10am-6pm, including set-up and take-down)
  • Day-time event 26-50 people: £750+VAT (10am-6pm, including set-up and take-down)
  • Evening event 0-25 people: £750+VAT (6pm-11pm, including set-up and take-down)
  • Evening event 26-50 people: £1000+VAT (6pm-11pm, including set-up and take-down)
  • reductions are possible in exchange for payment in kind
